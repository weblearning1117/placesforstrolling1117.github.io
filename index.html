Botanical Garden

Bogishamol street, 232 (Yunus-abad district)
Landmark: Tashkent Zoo
Phone number: (+998) 71-289-1052, (+998) 71-289-1052
Tashkent Botanical Garden is the second largest in the CIS. In 2011, the garden was visited by about 65 thousand people. The garden's collection is estimated at 6,000 species, forms and varieties of plants (mainly from the temperate zone), the number of species assessed in 2013 is 4,500. According to the Tashkent encyclopedia (1983), trees and shrubs account for 2,500 species (this figure reproduced in the "National Encyclopedia of Uzbekistan"), including 170 conifers, 2200 species of one, two- and perennial herbs

Landmark: Tashkent Zoo
Phone number: (+998) 71-289-1052, (+998) 71-289-1052

Japanese garden

Amir Temur street, 107 (Yunus-abad district)
Landmark: "INTERNATIONAL HOTEL TASHKENT"
Phone number: (+998) 71-238-5626
Work-time:10:00-19:00.
Price for entrance to the Japanese Garden in Tashkent Ticket for an adult - 20,000 soums. Ticket for pensioners - 10,000 soums.
Japanese Garden in Tashkent - a garden in the center of the capital, which has become one of the most magical sights cities. A peculiar Japanese island in the middle of a big city! Gazebo, tea house, lake, cozy wooden bridges, the diversity of birds - all this successfully conveys Japanese flavor. I want to come here meditate or walk, being surrounded by majestic greenery and peaceful birds.

Landmark: "INTERNATIONAL HOTEL TASHKENT"
Phone number: (+998) 71-238-5626
Work-time: 10:00-19:00.
